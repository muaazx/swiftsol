Privacy Policy

SwiftCare – Privacy Policy (Software Services Provider)

At SwiftCare, protecting your privacy is not just a legal requirement—it’s a core part of how we build trust with our clients. As a software services provider, we understand that businesses and individuals rely on us to handle sensitive information responsibly, securely, and transparently.

1. Information We Collect

SwiftCare collects only the information necessary to deliver high-quality software services. This may include:

  • Personal details such as name, email address, phone number, and company information when you contact us or use our services.
  • Technical data like IP address, browser type, device information, and usage behavior to improve system performance and user experience.
  • Project-related data shared by clients for development, testing, or deployment purposes.

We ensure that all data collection is lawful, fair, and limited to what is strictly required.

2. How We Use Your Information

The information we collect is used to:

  • Deliver and manage software development, maintenance, and support services.
  • Communicate with clients regarding updates, project progress, or service-related inquiries.
  • Improve our systems, security measures, and overall user experience.
  • Comply with legal and regulatory requirements.

We do not sell, rent, or misuse your personal or business data.

3. Data Protection and Security

SwiftCare takes strong security measures to protect your information from unauthorized access, loss, or misuse. These include:

  • Secure servers and encrypted communication channels.
  • Access controls ensuring only authorized personnel can view sensitive data.
  • Regular security audits and system monitoring.

While no system is completely immune to risks, we continuously upgrade our practices to maintain high standards of data protection.

4. Data Sharing and Third Parties

We only share your information when necessary and under strict conditions:

  • With trusted partners or service providers who assist in delivering our services (under confidentiality agreements).
  • When required by law, regulation, or legal processes.

We ensure that any third party handling your data follows proper security and privacy standards.

5. Client Data Ownership

All project-related data provided by clients remains their property. SwiftCare does not claim ownership of your business data, software content, or intellectual property. We only use it to fulfill agreed services.

6. Data Retention

We retain data only for as long as necessary to:

  • Complete the service or project.
  • Meet legal, tax, or compliance obligations.

After that, data is securely deleted or anonymized.

7. User Rights

Clients and users have the right to:

  • Access the personal data we hold about them.
  • Request correction of inaccurate information.
  • Request deletion of their data, where applicable.
  • Withdraw consent for certain data uses.

We respond to such requests promptly and transparently.

8. Cookies and Tracking Technologies

SwiftCare may use cookies or similar technologies on its website to:

  • Enhance user experience.
  • Analyze website performance and traffic.

Users can control cookie preferences through their browser settings.

9. Policy Updates

SwiftCare may update this privacy policy from time to time to reflect changes in technology, legal requirements, or business practices. Any updates will be clearly communicated through our website or official channels.

10. Contact Us

If you have any questions, concerns, or requests regarding your privacy or data, you can contact SwiftCare directly. We are committed to addressing your concerns professionally and promptly.


Final Note

At SwiftCare, privacy is built into everything we do. We aim to create secure, reliable, and transparent software solutions while ensuring that your data remains protected and under your control at all times.
